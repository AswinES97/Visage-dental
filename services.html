E-Max Veneers and Crowns

Whether you desire subtle smile enhancements or a complete smile makeover, E-Max versatility shines. E-Max crowns offer exceptional strength and durability, making them ideal for restoring cracked, damaged, or severely worn teeth. They seamlessly blend with your existing smile, eliminating unsightly metal lines and offering a natural, long-lasting solution.

E-Max Cad

E-Max CAD restorations boast exceptional translucency, mimicking the natural lightplay of your teeth for a remarkably lifelike appearance.
